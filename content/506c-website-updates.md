# FCG Website Updates — Rule 506(c), Global Compliance (v2)

This document specifies required edits to align the public website with a
Rule 506(c) Regulation D posture for a global private equity platform serving
institutional and other accredited investors.

---

## 1. HERO SECTION (PUBLIC, UNGATED)

### REMOVE (entirely from public pages)
- “Investment Opportunity”
- “We are raising…”
- “Fund I”
- Any references to:
  - returns
  - IRR
  - MOIC
  - bps uplift
  - allocations
  - timelines
  - capital amounts

### REPLACE WITH (exact copy)

**Headline**
> Private Markets Infrastructure Platform

**Subhead**
> Foresight Capital Group partners with institutional and other accredited investors to originate and execute infrastructure investments across energy, AI, and critical systems.

**Primary CTA**
> Investor Access

**Secondary CTA**
> Contact the Firm

---

## 2. INVESTOR ACCESS — REQUIRED INTERSTITIAL GATE

Trigger: Clicking “Investor Access”

### Display BEFORE any investment-related content

**Title**
> Accredited Investor Confirmation

**Body**
> Access to investment-related information is available only to institutional investors and other investors that qualify as “Accredited Investors” under applicable securities laws.
>
> By proceeding, you confirm that:
> - You are an institutional investor or otherwise qualify as an accredited investor under Rule 501(a) of Regulation D, and
> - You understand that any investment may be offered only pursuant to definitive offering documents.

**Controls**
- ☐ I confirm
- ☐ I do not qualify

**Buttons**
- Proceed to Verification
- Exit

No bypass. No scrolling past.

---

## 3. ACCREDITED INVESTOR VERIFICATION NOTICE (506(c))

Display wherever email capture, portal access, or next steps occur:

> Foresight Capital Group relies on Rule 506(c) of Regulation D and will take reasonable steps to verify accredited investor status prior to accepting any investment, including third-party verification where required.

---

## 4. CONTENT THAT MUST BE GATED (POST-VERIFICATION ONLY)

Remove from all public routes:
- Fund names or numbers (e.g., “Fund I”)
- Capital raising language
- Performance targets or projections
- IRR / MOIC / bps uplift
- Allocation or strategy percentages
- Offering timelines

Allowed only after:
1. Accredited investor confirmation
2. Verification process initiated
3. (Optional) NDA acceptance

---

## 5. FOOTER — REQUIRED LINKS

Add footer links:
- Terms of Use
- Investor Disclaimer
- Privacy Policy
- Regulatory Disclosures
- Contact

### Footer Disclaimer (exact copy)

> **Important Notice**
>
> This website is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation of an offer to buy any securities in any jurisdiction. Any offer of securities will be made only pursuant to definitive offering documents and only to institutional investors and other investors who are verified as accredited investors under Rule 506(c) of Regulation D and applicable non-U.S. laws.
>
> Access to investment-related information may be restricted by law. Persons who do not qualify should not attempt to access such information.

---

## 6. TERMS OF USE — REQUIRED SECTIONS

The Terms of Use must explicitly include:
- No offer / no solicitation
- Reliance on Rule 506(c)
- Institutional and accredited investor requirement
- Jurisdictional restrictions (non-U.S.)
- Forward-looking statements disclaimer
- No investment, legal, or tax advice
- Limitation of liability

---

## 7. GLOBAL COMPLIANCE LANGUAGE

Add to footer or first-page modal:

> This website is not directed to any person in any jurisdiction where access would be contrary to local law or regulation.

Optional (recommended):
- Geo-IP soft block for EU / UK / SG with disclaimer modal
