# Rule 506(c) Website Compliance Checklist (v2)

## Offering Structure
- [ ] Offering relies on Rule 506(c), not 506(b)
- [ ] All investors are institutional investors or otherwise qualify as accredited investors under Rule 501(a) of Regulation D
- [ ] Form D filed and kept current

## Website & Marketing
- [ ] Public pages contain NO fund-specific offering details
- [ ] No return targets, IRR, MOIC, or projections on public pages
- [ ] No “raising” or “investment opportunity” language publicly visible
- [ ] Brand / firm marketing clearly separated from fundraising

## Gating & Verification
- [ ] Accredited investor confirmation gate before fund content
- [ ] Reasonable steps taken to verify accredited investor status
- [ ] Verification records retained (CPA letter / third party / documents)

## Disclaimers & Legal
- [ ] Prominent “no offer / no solicitation” disclaimer
- [ ] Explicit Rule 506(c) reliance stated
- [ ] Jurisdictional restrictions disclosed
- [ ] Forward-looking statements disclaimer included

## Footer & Policies
- [ ] Terms of Use
- [ ] Investor Disclaimer
- [ ] Privacy Policy
- [ ] Regulatory Disclosures
- [ ] Contact information

## Global Considerations
- [ ] Website not directed to restricted jurisdictions
- [ ] Non-U.S. marketing posture reviewed (EU / UK / APAC)
- [ ] Geo-IP or disclaimer modal considered

## Records & Controls
- [ ] Versioned website content archived
- [ ] Marketing materials reviewed by counsel
- [ ] Investor communications logged
